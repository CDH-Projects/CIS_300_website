Who Am I?

I’m a Data Analytics student who’s really interested in how data can improve performance—whether that’s in business, operations, or even golf. I like digging into data, finding patterns, and figuring out what’s actually useful. A lot of what I learn comes from working on real projects and experimenting with different tools and ideas. This site is a mix of things I’ve learned, golf insights, and thoughts along the way.

Blog - This is where I will chit chat about random golf thoughts and how I am feeling about golf locations.
