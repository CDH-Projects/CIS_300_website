Who Am I?

I’m passionate about golf and the process of constantly improving. I enjoy analyzing my game, trying new strategies, and learning what actually makes a difference on the course. With a background in data analytics, I bring a bit of a data-driven mindset, but this site is mainly about golf—insights, experiences, and thoughts along the way.

What can I find on here?

Tips&Tricks - This is where I keep a record of all tips and tricks I have found helpful to improve my golf performance

Blog - This is where I will chit chat about random golf thoughts and how I am feeling about golf locations.

PGA Tournaments - This is where keep track of past and present tournaments, leaderboards, winnings, and highlighting of golf shots.
